FAQ

Can I train at home?

Yes, if you can keep the training intensity, you can do it. You have to do it the way that you feel better doing it.

How's the follow-up?

The follow-up it's based on seeing the evolution of your weight, strenght, based on your training, diet and sleeping. We adjust these last variables to make changes or adaptions.

I've never went to the gym, what if I make mistakes?

No one starts knowing. If your progress stops, we can adjust the intensity, check the technique of the exercises, or the diet. The most important thing is that you don't stop training, instead of being perfect.

Every plan contains a nutrition plan?

Yes, every plan includes it. The starter plan includes a basic nutrition plan according to your goal, i.e., to gain more muscular mass, definition and manteinance. The other plans, offer a custom nutrition plan, i.e., is adapted to your training, your daily activity, and your personal life.

When do I start seeing results?

Results appear progressively. However, after around 3 months, a noticeable change is usually visible. The longer you stay consistent, the greater your results will be.
